How Manifest reduces your team's workload in managing a 401(k) plan

Offering a 401(k) to your employees makes you a fiduciary.

Fiduciaries select investments, supervise employee contributions, overseeing admin chores, and a plethora of intricate duties.


Administering a 401(k) plan involves a wide range of tasks. You might need a JD, CFA, CPA, and a CFP to stay current.

How Manifest helps you reduce your administrative workload by 5x

Your team may spend anywhere from 8-40 hours a week administering your 401(k) plan.

Outsourcing a lot of the work to third parties can be helpful.

With Manifest, our product acts as a concierge extension to your current goals. So that your team can go back to doing what they do best.
